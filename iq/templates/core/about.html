💡 How It Works

Our AI system acts purely as a supportive learning aid. It enhances your learning experience by:

  • Processing uploaded academic notes to generate intelligent summaries
  • Extracting key concepts and definitions for quick revision
  • Creating practice questions based on your content
  • Analyzing content structure and providing improvement suggestions

⚠️ Note: The AI system does not replace faculty guidance or academic teaching and does not perform student evaluation or grading.
